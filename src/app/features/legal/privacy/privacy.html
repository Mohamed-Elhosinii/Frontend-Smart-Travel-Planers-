Your privacy is vital to us. This Privacy Policy details how TripMind collects, stores, uses, and protects your information when you use our smart travel solutions.

1. Information We Collect

To orchestrate customised travel experiences, we collect elements including:

  • Account Credentials: Your full name, email address, and password.
  • Travel Preferences: Budget limits, dates, preferred style indicators, and interactive chat prompts.
  • Local Caching Data: Your email address stored via local storage when "Remember Me" is enabled.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to refine planning tools and provide requested services:

  • Synthesising and routing itineraries through our backend planning engine.
  • Generating PDF files of your schedule and rendering interactive maps.
  • Sending password-retrieval alerts and confirming account status changes.

3. Caching and Storage Security

If you check "Remember me on this device", we store your email inside your browser's localStorage to expedite login. We advise caution, as no storage mechanism is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services & APIs

We integrate open mapping layers (Leaflet / OpenStreetMap) and photo catalogues (Unsplash). These external modules operate under separate privacy frameworks, and we encourage you to review their specific policies.

5. Your Controls

You can request account deletion, clear cached credentials from your local storage at any time, or modify your travel styles. Your preferences are adjustable directly in your settings.
